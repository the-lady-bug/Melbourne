We're here to help


Follow the steps below:

  1. Choose the criteria: Decide on whether you want to visit different Point of Interest places or Outdoor artworks. Select the appropriate options from the menus available.

  2. Places displayed: The map displays the different interesting places to visit in the chosen criteria.

  3. Tram Stop: Select the nearby tram stop to the place of interest. This will also display the tram timetable.

  4. Cafes: Selecting the tram stop also highlights the nearby eateries available to visit. These eateries are further classified based on cafes, and restaurants, pubs and takeaway food services.
